Contrasts in Kingdoms

Part 3 contrasts the kingdom mindset with the systems of the world, calling for repentance, identity renewal, and knowledge that drives light over darkness.

Repent

Repentance means to change your thinking. It is a reversal of the mind toward the kingdom of heaven that is now near.

  • Matthew 4:17 calls for a new way of thinking.
  • Change the conditioning of the mind toward kingdom truth.
  • Repentance is not just sorrow, it is a transformed mindset.
"As a man thinks in his heart, so is he." The kingdom begins in the mind of God and is released through renewed minds on earth.

You Are the Kingdom

The kingdom of God on earth is the functioning of rulers in their domain. You are designed for dominion and leadership by the spirit within you.

  • Dominion means to govern, rule, control, manage, master, and lead.
  • Leadership is to be discovered within, not manufactured outside.
  • God calls people by who they are becoming, not their current label.
  • You are a deployee, not an employee, assigned to bring kingdom culture.

Knowledge

Darkness is ignorance. The enemy uses ignorance to rule, but the word of God brings light and knowledge that breaks the grip of darkness.

  • The greatest power of the enemy is what you do not know.
  • The word of God is the light that destroys ignorance.
  • Study, read, and renew the mind to walk in light.
  • Romans 12:1-2 calls for transformation through renewed thinking.
The first thing God gave Adam to protect him was information. Knowledge is central to kingdom leadership and spiritual authority.
