The Kingdom Assignment of Jesus

Part 4 clarifies the mission of Jesus, the objectives for restoring the kingdom on earth, and the measurable outcomes of kingdom life.

Mission Statement

Bring back the kingdom of God to earth. Jesus preached the kingdom and built a family of sons and a commonwealth of citizens.

Matthew 4:17 anchors the mission: "Repent, for the kingdom of heaven has come near."

Objectives

The kingdom assignment includes clear objectives that restore citizens and ambassadors to their original calling.

  • Cleanse man as a temple to welcome the Governor.
  • Reconnect fallen ambassadors to the government.
  • Equip citizens to live the kingdom life.

KPIs

Each objective carries a measurable outcome that signals kingdom restoration in people.

  • Fallen sons cleansed by the finished work of the cross (John 19:30).
  • Holy Spirit breathed on disciples to empower ambassadors (John 20:22, Acts).
  • Sons taking care of the Father's business on earth (John 17:15).
